Detailed Description

template<CMesh TMesh, physics::CHyperElasticEnergy THyperElasticEnergy>
struct pbat::fem::HyperElasticPotential< TMesh, THyperElasticEnergy >

Total hyper elastic potential \( U(\mathbf{x}) = \int_\Omega \Psi(\mathbf{F}) d\Omega \).

where \( \mathbf{F} \) is the deformation gradient and \( \Psi \) is the hyper elastic energy density.

HyperElasticPotential's depends on a \( |Q| \)-point quadrature specified by element indices \( e_g \), quadrature weights \( w_g \), and shape function gradients \( \nabla \phi_g \) at quadrature points. This allows users to try out different quadrature rules seamlessly.
