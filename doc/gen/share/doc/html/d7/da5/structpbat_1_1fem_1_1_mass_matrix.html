template<CMesh TMesh, int QuadratureOrder>
struct pbat::fem::MassMatrix< TMesh, QuadratureOrder >

A matrix-free representation of a finite element mass matrix \( \mathbf{M}_{ij} = \int_\Omega \rho(X) \phi_i(X) \phi_j(X) \).
