template<pbat::math::CLinearOperator Lhs, typename Rhs, int ProductType>
struct Eigen::internal::generic_product_impl< Lhs, Rhs, SparseShape, DenseShape, ProductType >

See Eigen/src/Core/util/Constants.h, we want to specialize all product types.

enum ProductImplType { DefaultProduct = 0, LazyProduct, AliasFreeProduct, CoeffBasedProductMode, LazyCoeffBasedProductMode, OuterProduct, InnerProduct, GemvProduct, GemmProduct };

This way, our matrix-free linear operators will be able to operate on all kinds of matrix/vector arguments, not just dynamic sized ones, as the GemvProduct denotes.
