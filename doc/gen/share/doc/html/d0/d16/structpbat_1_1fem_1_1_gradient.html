Detailed Description

template<CMesh TMesh>
struct pbat::fem::Gradient< TMesh >

Represents an FEM gradient operator \( \mathbf{G} \in \mathbb{R}^{dg \times n} \).

Given a function \( u(X) \) discretized at mesh nodes \( X_i \)

\[\nabla u(X) = \sum_j u_j \nabla \phi_j(X) \; \forall \; e \in E \]

where \( \nabla \phi_j(X) \) is the gradient of the shape function \( \phi_j(X) \) at element \( e \).

The gradient operator \( \mathbf{G} \) is thus defined as the matrix

\[\mathbf{G}^k = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial \phi_i}{\partial X_k} \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n} \]

where \( n \) is the number of nodes in the mesh and \( 1 \leq k \leq d \) is a spatial dimension of the domain.

\[\mathbf{G} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{G}^1 \\ \vdots \\ \mathbf{G}^d \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{dg \times n} \]

where \( g \) is the number of evaluation points.
